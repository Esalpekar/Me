AIXI approximator

AIXI (Hutter 2005) is a formalization of a universal, ideal, and uncomputable agent. It maximizes a reward metric in an unknown environment. I asked: How many practical deviations from this ideal must be taken to achieve a functional agent?

Keeping tabs on every deviation, I adapted AIXI to work through tasks using LLMs and a continuously updated model of its environment:

A constitution serves as the agent's moral and productive guide. A judge evaluates each of the agent's actions against the constitution, and returns a detailed report. This is the reward percept.

Tools are recast as "sub-environments:" self-manifested models of the true environment which are sampled to receive an observation.

A key philosophy of this project is that the data flow is entirely text-based. No attempts are made to compress data into quantitative metrics: essays are low-density but high-fidelity, and attention mechanisms do a better job of picking out the meaningful stuff than I can. Optimization then becomes solely a compression challenge.

Custom Backtesting Engine

A simple backtesting engine on which I test predictive trading strategies. Pulls historical data from a variety of sources, and returns a variety of plots and stats in a cute little HTML report.

Existing backtesting engines like LEAN aren't tailored to reporting the intrinsic metrics of ML models. For my workflow, I wanted to get feedback on both my Pytorch skills and my finance skills simultaneously.

Billing Data Cleaner and Workspace

As an intern at OxBlue, I saw a problem: at the top of each month, senior accountants were spending a full day cleaning up malformed billing reports so that they could be uploaded to our accounting software. This was tedious, so I automated it.

The program fixes the formulaic issues and flags data that needs human attention to resolve. Remaining data is organized into an Excel workspace with a macro-powered UI for easy processing.

Uploading now takes one accountant a few hours at most, an estimated 70% reduction in effort.
